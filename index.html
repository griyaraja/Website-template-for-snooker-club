Rules

Snooker Club Rules:

  1. Rule 1: Snooker is played with 22 balls on a green cloth-covered table.
  2. Rule 2: Players aim to pot red balls followed by colored balls to score points.
  3. Rule 3: Fouls result in penalty points, and players strategize to build high-scoring breaks.
